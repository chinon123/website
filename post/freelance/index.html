@FREELANCE - How to deal with the preparation of a seminary at COP21 after a dreadful event ?

Apr 02, 2015

What a great mission to work for the preparation of a seminar at COP21 for CNRS - national scientific research center.

Abstract

Project management is not something I am familiar with. COP21 summit is a big deal and its preparation not easy. The mission's title is assistance for the implementation of a corporate philanthropy research policy for the financing of phase 2 of the Global Youth Climate Pact.

In the context of a dramatic event in Paris on November the 13th 2015, my contribution is focused on global management for numeric communication.

Renewing the seminar was a real challenge and distinguishing oneself from other environmental projects: a successful effort and an accomplished mission thanks to versatility.
