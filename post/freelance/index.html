One of the two services provided is to assist for the implementation of a corporate philanthropy research policy for a Global Youth Climate Pact for a cluster of research Center including CNRS - National Center for Scientific Research which leed to a seminary at COP21.

Abstract

The Global Youth Climate Pact is a process of citizen participation and an exercise in international cooperation on the theme of environmental education among young people. The strategy is to reduce environmental injustice through grassroots awareness for better public actions in favour of the climate.

Before starting the mission, I didn't know much about managing a project of this scale. The COP21 framework is demanding and its preparation was not easy. The attacks in Paris on 13 November 2015 also changed the content of the mission. So I was involved in the coordination of the players and communication.

I coordinated the actors and managed the communication until its completion and finally participated in its restitution at COP21.
