Internship made at SNCF in collaboration with l'Institut de la Région Île-de-France regarding the impact of 2 new lines, today and in 2030.

Abstract

This forecasting study is helping decision regarding trains frequency, railway station localisation and dimension.

Two keys variables are checked: population and employment regarding time and geography. They help us to ask the corrects questions :

  • is it the line offering the right evolution regarding macro urban planning document strategy? new lines have to be compatible all together
  • is the line typology in respect with tomorrow's population and employment streams? suburbs trains are offering a different service than subway
  • how is the line impacting all cross areas? is the district of la Défense not taking all the benefit from others ?
