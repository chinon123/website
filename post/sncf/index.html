@SNCF - How to size new line regarding Paris' evolution in 2030 ?

Jan 01, 2014

Internship made at SNCF in collaboration with l'Institut de la Région Île-de-France regarding the impact of 2 new lines of metro, today and in 2030.

Abstract

This forecasting study is helping decision regarding : trains frequency, railway station localisation and dimension.

Two keys variables are checked : population and employement regarding time and geography. They help us to ask the corrects questions :

  • is the model using the right hypothesis ? birth rate, employement, job localisation and rate of evolution
  • is it the line offering the right evolution regarding macro urbain planing document strategy ? new lines have to be compatible all together
  • is the line typology in respect with tomorrow's population and employement streams ? suburbs trains is offering a different service than subway
  • how the line is impacting all cross areas ? is the area of la Défense not taking all the benefit from others ?
