@MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT - How to urban plan with data science ?

Jan 01, 2018

At the ministry of Environment and more specially at the local administration of Val d'Oise department, I learn to manage and to tackle local housing policy

Abstract

The depatemental level has the duty to track records and applience of a large sets of juridiction. About local housing policy it goes from urban planning, house quality to allocation of social housing.

As an assistant head of a team of 5, I had the opportunity to follow 3 items :

  • SRU : a municipality with a certain amount of population has the duty to have a minimum of social housing. This law is aiming urban diversity
  • PLH : macro-level urban document to forecast the implementation of housing policy
  • GDV : a municipality with a certain amount of population has the duty to have a minimum of equipment to supply the demand of stay from gypsies

Moreover, as a head of studies, I had the mission to fulfill 3 studies specific to the Val d'Oise situation :

  • housing of senior citizen : to finance senior house through social housing tools, the demand from real estate promoter is not corresponding to the reality of the demand.
  • sale of property for future completion : compare to normal acquisition, a sale of property for future completion is increasing the value of the rent in social housing.
  • and suburban division : in some particular situation, a citizen can divide his parcel and erect houses. One effect of this possibility is the anormal density of population in some areas and the suscpicion of slumlord/

Thanks to these 2 functions, I have designed and produced an automatic territorial obsvervatory for fast KPI's.
