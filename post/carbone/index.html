@CARBONE 4 - How to price carbone ?

Jan 01, 2015

After graduation, I have join a consulting firm Carbone 4, specialised in climate change strategy. One of the three mission is carbone price and its strategic involment for future law applience.

Abstract

To tackle future reglementation which can impact negatively business, the client is considering to implement willingly a carbon price mechanism.

CDP (carbon display program) runs the global disclosure system to manage and measure environmental impact. It give a description of the current situation at a microeconomy level and trends :

  • does a company is already tackling future reglementation regarding its business ?
  • does a company is volunteer to adapt a carbone price mechanism ?

On the other hand, it also show the situation at global level :

  • is a country already implementing a carbon price for its differents sectors ?
  • how the carbone price is made and evolving ?
  • what is the model used beyind it ?

In a nutshell, at a macro level, all the differents studies shows a rise in carbon reglementation and its price. The mission was to give tools for the client to defuse this business thread.
