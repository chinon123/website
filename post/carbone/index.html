@CARBONE 4 - How to price carbone ?

Jan 01, 2015

After graduation, I have joined a consulting firm Carbone 4, specialised in climate change strategy. One of the three mission is carbon price and its strategic involvement for future law appliance.

Abstract

A client is considering a carbon price mechanism to tackle future law regulation.

CDP (carbon display program) runs the global disclosure system to manage and measure environmental impact. It gives a description of the current situation at a microeconomy level and trends :

  • does a company is already tackling future regulation regarding its business?
  • does a company is volunteer to adopt a carbon price mechanism?

On the other hand, it also shows the situation at the global level :

  • is a country already implementing a carbon price for its different sectors?
  • how is the carbon price made and evolving?
  • what is the model used behind it?

In a nutshell, at a macro level, all the different studies show a rise in carbon regulation and its price. The mission was to give tools for the client to defuse this business thread.
