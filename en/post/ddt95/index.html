Jan 01, 2018fr

public policyurban planningRBusiness ObjectMS Officebibliographical research

At the ministry of Environment and more especially at the local administration of Val d'Oise department, I learn to manage and to tackle local housing policy.

Abstract

The departmental level has the duty to track records and appliance of a large set of jurisdiction. About local housing policy, it goes from urban planning, house quality to the allocation of social housing.

As an assistant head of a team of 5, I had the opportunity to follow three items :

  • SRU: a municipality with a certain amount of population has the duty to have a minimum of social housing. This law is aiming for urban diversity
  • PLH: macro-level urban document to forecast the implementation of housing policy
  • GDV: a municipality with a certain amount of population has the duty to have a minimum of equipment to supply the demand to stay from gipsies.

Moreover, as a head of studies, I had the mission to fulfil three studies specific to the Val d'Oise situation :

  • housing of senior citizen : to finance senior house through social housing tools, the demand from real estate promoter is not corresponding to the reality of the demand.
  • sale of property for future completion : compare to normal acquisition, a sale of property for future completion is increasing the value of the rent in social housing.
  • and suburban division : in some particular situation, a citizen can divide his parcel and erect houses. One effect of this possibility is the anormal density of population in some areas and the suspicion of slumlord/

Thanks to these two functions, I have designed and produced an automatic territorial observatory for fast KPI's.
